## Plugin Recommendations
- **Advanced Forms:** Use a form plugin with conditional logic (e.g., Typeform or Gravity Forms) to capture tailored project briefs from prospects.
- **Analytics Connector:** Integrate a connector like Fivetran or Zapier to sync app data directly into the dashboard framework.
- **Accessibility Checker:** Employ a plugin such as Stark or axe DevTools to audit color contrast and keyboard navigation before launch.

## Integrations Section
- **Slack Notifications:** Send automated insights and alert summaries to dedicated Slack channels for executive and operations teams.
- **CRM Sync:** Keep Salesforce or HubSpot opportunities aligned with dashboard KPIs using scheduled data pulls.
- **Project Management:** Connect Asana or Jira to convert insight-driven action items into trackable tasks.
- **Data Warehouses:** Support secure connections to Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift for governed analytics at scale.

## Motion Ideas
- Subtle hero background gradient that shifts based on scroll position to evoke live data refreshes.
- Animated KPI counters that increment to their live values when they enter the viewport.
- Slide-in case study cards triggered by intersection observers to emphasize results-focused storytelling.

## Governance Notes
- Establish quarterly data quality audits led by analytics and IT stakeholders to validate source accuracy.
- Define role-based access controls for dashboard editing, with multi-factor authentication for administrators.
- Maintain a documented change-log covering dataset modifications, visualization updates, and plugin additions for compliance reviews.
- Provide a centralized knowledge base outlining metric definitions, data refresh cadences, and escalation paths.
