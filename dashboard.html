What is the Farm to Scale Operating System?

The Farm to Scale OS is a framework that identifies, diagnoses, and eliminates critical revenue leaks that prevent sustainable customer growth—so expansion becomes predictable and repeatable.

What are the three critical revenue leaks?

The Handoff Leak (Sales-to-CS execution breaks), the Value Gap Leak (expected outcomes diverge from perceived value), and the Silent Leak (low-engagement accounts evaluate alternatives without triggering risk signals).

How is the OS implemented inside a team?

Through four phases: Diagnostic (Leak Assessment), Design (processes, playbooks, metrics), Implementation (hands-on embedding), and Scale (repeatable expansion after leaks are resolved).

What should leaders expect in the first 90 days?

Clear renewal ownership, reduced last-minute firefighting, stronger forecast visibility, and a repeatable expansion motion rooted in customer outcomes.
